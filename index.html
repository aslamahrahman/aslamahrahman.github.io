Welcome to my portfolio!

I am Aslamah Rahman. I am a recent Masters graduate from the University of Pennsylvania (May 2020), majoring in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Mechatronics and Robotic Systems. I obtained my B. Tech. from Indian Institute of Technology Madras in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering in July 2018

Throughout my higher education and career pursuits, I have had the opportunity to dabble in several different engineering disciplines and to explore and create opportunities at their intersection. Which is why when people ask me what do I do, I end up replying "everything engineering" which is an easier answer than saying "materials science - computational modeling - web application development - supply chain management - mechatronics - machine learning - product design - embedded programming and counting". This is a showcase of the concoction of projects I have pursued and absolutely loved being a part of. I believe that answers to the toughest problems out there already exists, and they live at the intersection of disciplines. This is what I hope to explore and achieve through my academics and career.
